Intel Briefing

How to Play

Select an Operative for each sector that meets the criteria for both the Row and Column value. Categories are independent, meaning the operative must simply possess both traits separately.

Example: If categories are Straw Hat Pirates and Devil Fruit User, the operative must belong to that series/group and possess a Devil Fruit power.

Operative Recon

There are no limits on guesses per sector. If a scan fails, that operative will be blacklisted for that sector. You can change a submitted operative by clicking the square again, even after completion.

Rarity Ranks

An operative's rarity is determined by their Tag Density in the database. Operatives with fewer tags are rarer and higher ranked:

Genin (> 12 Tags)
Chunin (11-12 Tags)
Jonin (9-10 Tags)
Anbu (7-8 Tags)
Kage (5-6 Tags)
Legendary Sannin (0-4 Tags)

Recon Tools

Series Recon: View a stream of all anime series currently active in the grid sectors.

Archive: Travel back through previous mission logs to view or play past grids.
